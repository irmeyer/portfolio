Flask/Python/SQL Application Description

The Outdoor Adventures Trip Management Website is a Flask-based application designed for the Outdoor Adventures Company to manage trips and members efficiently. This system allows the company to create, edit, and delete trip and member records, ensuring seamless organization of adventure trips.

Key Features:

Homepage (/)

  • Displays the Outdoor Adventures logo, a brief site description, and navigation buttons to view trips and members.

Member Management (/members/)

  • Lists all members of the Outdoor Adventuring Club, including name, address, email, date of birth, and phone number.
  • Data is pulled from members.csv and sorted by age, with the oldest members displayed first.

Trip Management (/trips/)

  • Displays all available trips with name, start date, duration, location, and cost, sorted by start date (oldest first).
  • Data is sourced from trips.csv, ensuring consistency and ease of access.
  • Each trip name links to its individual details page.

Individual Trip Pages (/trips//)

  • Displays detailed trip information, including activity level, leader, and additional trip description.
  • Allows users to view trip specifics before signing up.

Data Handling & Structure:

  • All trip and member data is stored in CSV files (trips.csv, members.csv), ensuring scalability and easy data management.
  • A minimum of six trips and six members must be present, with complete data for each.
