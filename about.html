About Me

Hi, I'm Bella Meyer! I'm a software engineer with a passion for blending technical expertise with thoughtful design. With experience in full-stack development, cybersecurity, and graphic design, I bring a well-rounded perspective to every project.

I'm graduating from Indiana University's Luddy School of Informatics, Engineering, and Computing with a B.S. in Informatics, complemented by minors in Web Development, Information Security, and Graphic Design. My ability to bridge the gap between aesthetics and functionality allows me to create secure, visually compelling, and user-friendly digital experiences.

Beyond coding, I'm highly organized, professional, and naturally take on leadership roles in collaborative settings. Whether crafting intuitive interfaces, optimizing system security, or leading a team, I thrive at the intersection of creativity and technology.
